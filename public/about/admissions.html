Getting Involved With RMU

We are not currently accepting new students, but stay tuned for announcements of new entrance exams and other public events on our news page. While you're waiting, you can meet with current RMU participants and discuss the program via the rmu-talk mailing list, or the #rmu IRC channel on Freenode.

We occasionally hold events that are open to the public, such as meetings with guest speakers or study sessions hosted by our staff and alumni. These events will also be announced on our news page, so be sure to keep an eye on it if you'd like to get involved.

Feel free to contact us if you have any questions
